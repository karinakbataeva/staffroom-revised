# Staffroom — Product Improvement Case Study

## Improving teacher workplace insights through better product experience

Staffroom is an existing teacher workplace review platform that helps educators understand school work environments.

This project focuses on improving the existing Staffroom experience by making workplace information easier to understand, more actionable, and more useful for teachers evaluating career opportunities.

---

# 🎯 Product Context

Teachers often struggle to evaluate schools as employers.

While workplace reviews provide valuable information, users still face challenges:

- Too many reviews to read
- Difficulty identifying recurring themes
- Ratings without enough context
- Limited understanding of strengths and trade-offs

The goal of this improvement project was to reduce information overload and help teachers make faster, better-informed decisions.

# 🔍 Problem Areas Identified

## 1. Ratings lacked context

Before:


Infrastructure: 4.6
Culture: 4.0
Salary: 4.3


Users could see scores but had limited understanding of what those numbers meant.

### Improvement:

Added benchmark indicators:


Infrastructure

4.6

🏆 Top 10% among Delhi schools

██████████████████░░


This helps users interpret ratings instead of guessing.

---

## 2. Reviews required too much reading

Teachers needed to spend significant time reading multiple reviews to identify patterns.

### Improvement:

Introduced structured AI summaries:

### Teachers appreciate:
- High-achieving students
- Excellent facilities
- Supportive colleagues
- Career value

### Teachers cautioned:
- Heavy paperwork
- Long working hours
- Administrative workload

---

## 3. Workplace information was difficult to scan

Users evaluating jobs need quick signals.

### Improvement:

Created a clearer workplace snapshot:

- School culture
- Career growth
- Salary satisfaction
- Infrastructure
- Management
- Workload balance

Each metric includes:
- Rating
- Explanation
- Benchmark context

# 🚀 Key Improvements

## AI-Powered Summary Experience

Converted unstructured workplace feedback into actionable insights.

Value:
- Reduced reading time
- Highlighted recurring themes
- Improved decision confidence

---

## Contextual Ratings

Added comparison indicators to make scores meaningful.

Instead of:

"Infrastructure: 4.6"

Users see:

"4.6 — Top 10% among Delhi schools"

---

## Improved Information Hierarchy

Reorganized the page around the user's decision journey:

1. Overall workplace impression
2. AI summary
3. Strengths and concerns
4. Workplace metrics
5. Detailed reviews
